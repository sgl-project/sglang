About the job
About The Role

We are seeking a Senior AI Agent Engineer to join a fast-moving digital transformation team at a leading organization. This is a hands-on role designing and deploying agentic AI systems that deliver real-world impact for customers. You will be working on sophisticated AI workflows, from prompt architecture and multi-step reasoning agents to serverless orchestration using AWS-native technologies.

This is an opportunity to join a small, collaborative product team at a state-of-the-art innovation center, building safe, scalable, and efficient AI solutions that will shape the future of customer-facing systems.

Key Responsibilities

 Design and implement agentic AI solutions using AWS Bedrock, including Agents, Knowledge Bases, and Guardrails
 Build and orchestrate serverless workflows with Lambda, Step Functions, and EventBridge
 Develop advanced prompt strategies for multi-step reasoning and autonomous agents (ReAct, chain-of-thought, plan-and-execute, reflection)
 Integrate AI agents with APIs, tools, and external resources
 Build frameworks to evaluate agent performance, reliability, and safety
 Collaborate with software engineers, designers, and product owners to deliver fully integrated solutions
 Mentor junior team members and share best practices in AI and software engineering

Technical Requirements / Skills

 Strong experience in prompt engineering and building autonomous AI agents
 Hands-on with AWS Bedrock, Python, LangChain, and serverless services (Lambda, Step Functions, API Gateway, DynamoDB, S3)
 Experience with Terraform and modern CI/CD practices
 Strong understanding of API integration, tool-use patterns, and database optimization
 Solid software development experience in Python or Java (5–9 years total experience preferred)
 Familiarity with functional programming concepts, software design patterns, and cybersecurity principles

This is a great opportunity for engineers looking to work at the forefront of AI agent development in a production environment, contributing to transformative digital initiatives while working with a collaborative, high-performing team.
