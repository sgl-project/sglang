# BNSF | Tech - Data & AI Engineer Job Description

## Company Overview

**BNSF Railway** is one of the largest U.S. railroads transporting the nation's freight across 28 western states and 3 Canadian provinces. As a member of our team, you will play a role in supporting the movement of essential products and materials that help feed, clothe, supply, and power communities throughout America and the world.

**BNSF | Tech** is the technology division making BNSF the preeminent freight and mobility company in north america.

## Key Responsibilities

### AI Development and Implementation

- Assist in designing, developing, and implementing AI models and algorithms tailored to railroad operations, including:
  - Predictive maintenance
  - Scheduling
  - Safety monitoring
- Collaborate with senior engineers to apply machine learning techniques to analyze large datasets and extract actionable insights

### Data Collection and Analysis

- Collect, preprocess, and clean data from various sources (e.g., sensors, GPS, maintenance logs) for training AI models
- Conduct exploratory data analysis to identify trends and patterns relevant to rail operations
- Building and maintaining Extract, Transform, Load (ETL) pipelines for AI applications

### Optimization and Automation

- Work in partnership with data scientists to develop AI-driven solutions to optimize:
  - Train scheduling
  - Route planning
  - Fuel efficiency
- Support the deployment of automation tools for monitoring and managing railroad systems

### Testing and Validation

- Participate in testing and optimization of AI models to ensure accuracy, reliability, and compliance with safety standards
- Monitor model performance and assist in troubleshooting issues as they arise

### Collaboration and Communication

- Work closely with cross-functional teams, including:
  - Data scientists
  - Software engineers
  - Business analysts
- Understand their needs and enable AI-driven solutions
- Communicate technical concepts and project updates to both technical and non-technical stakeholders

### Continuous Learning and Development

- Stay up-to-date with advancements in AI, machine learning, and railroad technologies
- Participate in training sessions, workshops, and conferences to enhance skills and knowledge
