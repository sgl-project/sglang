## Team Mission

The Health Sensing HW team develops groundbreaking health sensors that are central to helping our customers live a healthier lifestyle.

---

## Role Summary

We are looking for a **data engineer** with a desire to innovate in the space of optical sensors and health sensing features, with a passion for data pipelines/infrastructure and software development.

### Key Focus Areas

- Develop system models of one or more health sensors to assess the performance of the sensor and inform design decisions
- Create tools to consume data from cloud-based databases, run data quality analyses, and root cause outliers
- Build tools to streamline the data analyses with existing DL/ML algorithms
- Work closely with internal and cross-functional teams to tackle challenges involving sensors, algorithms, and user behavior
- Play a key role in developing and quantifying the performance of hardware solutions to enable optical sensing features

---

## Team Description

The team you will join is responsible for **designing and delivering health features for Apple's wearable devices**. We are highly collaborative and partner with a variety of teams across Apple.

---

## Responsibilities

### 1. Requirements and Tool Development

- Solicit and consolidate requirements from sensor engineers for:
  - Algorithm evaluation
  - Behavioral modeling
  - Data quality analyses
  - Outlier analyses
  - Factory data analysis tools
- Develop and maintain these tools/codebases/pipelines and consolidate common blocks

### 2. Data Management and Analysis

- Manage and analyze data from user studies to identify key behaviors and establish relevant dependencies
- Produce / present meaningful data visualizations to the team

### 3. Design Evaluation and Communication

- Evaluate the impact of design choices on engineering metrics
- Propose trade-offs
- Communicate the results and insights to cross-functional teams

### 4. Software Engineering

- Maintain simulation code with best software engineering practices
- Optimize and refactor codebase when necessary for performance and readability

### 5. System Performance Prediction

- Use developed code to predict system/user performance across a range of operating conditions including:
  - HW/FW settings
  - User physiology
  - Ambient conditions
  - etc.
