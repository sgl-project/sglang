Attention Zones ? Tokens are classified into attention zones based on their fingerprint - a 20-dimensional vector capturing the attention distribution pattern.
Semantic Bridge Mid-range attention (17-48 tokens back) with high entropy. Model connecting concepts across context. mid_mass > 0.4 && entropy > 0.5
Syntax Floor Local attention (1-16 tokens) with low entropy. Focused on recent context for syntax/structure. local_mass > 0.6 && entropy < 2.5
Exploration Diffuse attention with high entropy. Model uncertain, exploring multiple possibilities. total_mass < 0.5 && entropy > 3.0
Steering Long-range attention (49+ tokens back). Model looking at instructions or early context. long_mass > 0.4
